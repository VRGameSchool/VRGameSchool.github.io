Meet Your Instructors:


Jason Mathias

Unity & VR Instructor

Jaosn holds a Master's Degree from USC's Interactive Media and Games division of the School of Cinematic Arts. His work has been featured in educational games conferences around the country, and he has developed games for major companies such as Toyota and VR Playhouse. In addition to founding Game School, he consults on business strategy and does Product Management at rhubarb studios in downtown Los Angeles.

James Calhoun

Film & Code Instructor

James made his first film when he was nineteen and premiered it at the Warner Grand Theatre in LA. Shortly after, he then started his first company making commercials for local businesses to fund his creative endeavors. His first tech startup FADM was a crowdfunding platform for filmmakers. Today James works as a consultant with technology startups in software engineering, product management, business development, and investment.
