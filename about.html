What We Do

Game School was founded on the idea that anyone can learn to make Virtual Reality. We're game designers and self-taught coders, and we want to make the next generation of influencers in the world of Virtual Reality. There are tons of places that VR can go - from virtual tourism, to therapy, to training - and we want to help anyone who has an idea, to understand how to make it a (virtual) reality.
